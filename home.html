What is MyTrickster?


MyTrickster takes place in a shared low fantasy setting where the human species have technology, such as steam power, but continue to study the ruins of a long-past advanced civilization. In the the less populated regions, monsters roam the landscape and threaten small villages or research bases that have been established to study the ruins and these monsters. Take the role of a Hunter that serves to help protect the villages and bases from these monsters, typically aiding in researching these.

EXPLORE DRAKORIA

In the far reaches of Drakoria, an ancient heavenly event known as the The Rampage has consumed powerful monsters and transformed animals,
driven by fear, these creatures threaten the peaceful coexistence of mankind and the other species that call the continent home.
